About 7Skill Academy

Learn about our journey, mission, and the team behind 7Skill Academy.

Our Story

7Skill Academy was founded in 2018 with a vision to bridge the digital skills gap and empower individuals and businesses to thrive in the digital landscape.

What started as a small training center has now evolved into a comprehensive digital education and AI automation hub, serving clients from over 20 countries worldwide.

Our unique approach of providing personalized 1:1 training has helped thousands of students transform their careers and businesses by mastering digital marketing skills and implementing cutting-edge AI solutions.

Our Mission

At 7Skill Academy, our mission is to democratize digital skills education and make advanced AI automation accessible to businesses of all sizes.

We believe that personalized education and tailored automation solutions are the keys to success in the digital age. That's why we focus on providing 1:1 training and custom AI implementations that address the specific needs of each student and client.

Our goal is to empower 1 million individuals and businesses by 2030, helping them harness the power of digital marketing and AI to achieve sustainable growth and success.

Our Values

Personalized Learning

We believe that every individual learns differently, which is why we provide personalized 1:1 training tailored to each student's pace and learning style.

Innovation

We constantly innovate our training methods and automation solutions to stay ahead of the rapidly evolving digital landscape and provide cutting-edge education.

Partnership

We see ourselves as partners in our clients' success, working collaboratively to achieve their goals and providing ongoing support beyond the training period.
